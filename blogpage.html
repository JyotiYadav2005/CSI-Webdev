Flying High

When does life allow you to "waste" time in thinking?
Me and him recently finished watching Sex Education Season 4 and although I wasn't really sure about the show a few months back when he asked me whether I wanted to watch it or not. I am kind of glad I did. As some of my friends would sarcastically say "How romantic" when I tell them Changes are inevitable. The realization hits as you slowly see the change taking over the way you wanted your life to be. As a kid we all have this wish to grow up, which only becomes another wish to be a kid again. During the teenage years, I saw many phases of myself as well as my friend's life. Before understanding the “Monday Blues” there’s always a time when blue is the best color our eyes can see right up in the sky. Seeing the birds fly, sunrises and sunsets, the mesmerizing night sky filled with the stars and a bright spot of beauty - the moon. Every little child at least once falls in love with the world miles above their head. Meanwhile The silence hits hard when its the loudspeaker of the house going quiet. Everyone is sad when the youngest or the jolliest person of the family/group is sad. "You cannot be stressing over it, today is such a happy day we need you in your best mood." "Oh please get over it you have better things to spend your energy in".
